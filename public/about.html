About Me

My name is Taylor Lamorie, and I am originally from Sault Ste. Marie, Ontario, Canada. I graduated in May 2022 with a bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering and a minor in Mathematics from Lake Superior State University in Sault Ste. Marie, Michigan. My passion has always been learning new programming languages and techniques. I have a keen interest in airplanes, and before pursuing a career in engineering, I attended flight school with the goal of becoming a certified pilot. However, I discovered that I enjoyed the mathematics and engineering aspects involved in airplane development even more.

I have a genuine interest to further develop my programming skills and knowledge and am seeking an opportunity that will allow me to learn new programming languages and to challenge myself in the application of new software in innovative projects, while being engaged in problem-solving and troubleshooting. I am committed to completing projects with attention to both detail and quality, recognizing and appreciating the value of persevering, of learning from what did not work, and of experimenting with new ideas and modifications.

On a personal level, I am a mature and responsible individual who works well both independently and as a team, and who builds positive relationships and rapport with colleagues. I have excellent organization and time management skills and understand the importance of completing tasks even when faced with competing priorities. I believe in keeping both my mind and my body healthy, thus I enjoy engaging in personal fitness, golf, hockey and soccer to keep myself physically fit, and also seek new learning opportunities to grow, challenge and improve myself as a learner and employee.
